Royal, true to its name, signifies richness of taste and flavour. The taste of Royal is one that is invigorating for the heart and mind.It enlivens the spirit with its flavour and freshness.

This brand also is available in two standards, Gold and Silver. Royal has been launched in the states of Punjab, Madhya Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh, Andhra Pradesh, Rajasthan, Delhi & Himachal Pardesh.
