Under the leadership of Mr. Gopal Poddar (Chairman & M.D.), Mr. Shankar Poddar (Director) & Mr. Subhas Poddar (Director), along with a professional team and experienced factory workers, the company successfully ventured into the biscuit industry, & set up a state of the art fully automatic factory at Asansol, W.B. Limtex offers different varieties of biscuits like Super Marie, Morning Marie, Classic Marie, Makhan Bhog, Milk Maza, Ener-G, Hot Choice, Cream Cracker, Double Bite, Coco Bite, Snakes, Orange Cream, etc.
