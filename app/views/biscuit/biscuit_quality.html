Raw materials (ingredients) are delivered just in time for production. When raw materials are received on site, they are inspected for cleanliness and quality then released to the storage area.

  • Ingredients are weighed and added to the mixer. The size of the batch is pre-planned to meet expected consumer demand.

  • The ingredients are then mixed together and the dough is formed. The dough is then removed from the mixer and transferred to the production line.
